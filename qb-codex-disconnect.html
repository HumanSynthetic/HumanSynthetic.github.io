Company Disconnected

This page confirms that a QuickBooks company connection has been disconnected or is in the process of being disconnected.

After disconnect:

  • the integration should no longer request new QuickBooks data for that company
  • stored connection tokens should be revoked or removed where applicable
  • reconnection requires a fresh authorization flow

If the disconnection was unintentional, return to the connect page and start the authorization flow again.
