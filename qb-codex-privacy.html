Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This QuickBooks integration is operated by Jared Hull. This policy describes what information may be accessed through the integration, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information Collected

  • OAuth tokens and technical connection metadata required to authenticate with QuickBooks
  • QuickBooks company data specifically required to perform the requested integration tasks
  • Basic operational logs needed for troubleshooting, auditability, and service reliability

How Information Is Used

  • To establish and maintain a QuickBooks connection
  • To read or update authorized accounting data for the intended integration workflow
  • To debug connection issues and maintain service integrity

Data Sharing

Data accessed through the integration is not sold. Data is only shared with service providers or infrastructure components required to operate the integration, or when required by law.

Data Retention

Connection metadata and logs are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operation, troubleshooting, security, and recordkeeping. QuickBooks data is not retained longer than needed for the authorized workflow.

Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures are used to protect tokens, credentials, and connected accounting data. No internet-connected system is guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

Disconnect and Revocation

If a company disconnects the integration, access tokens and any related connection state should be revoked or removed as part of the disconnect workflow.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact path provided on the main site or integration landing page.
